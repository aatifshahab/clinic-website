About Our Clinic

The Vision and Vitality Center was founded with the mission of improving the quality of life for individuals experiencing vision loss. We also emphasize child rehabilitation, recognizing that early intervention is crucial for children with neurodevelopmental challenges such as Autism, ADHD, and Sensory Processing Disorder.
